Here we see how photons reflect off of curved surfaces according to Snell's Law. The parabolic reflection is interesting because we can see the geometric role of the focus of the parabola.

Instructions: Click the mouse inside the black area to create a line of incoming photons. Watch as they reflect off of the parabolic surface and pass through the focus of the parabola.
